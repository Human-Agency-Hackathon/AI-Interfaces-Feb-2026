✦ The Method ✦

How It Works

I
Spawn
Issue a command to Claude Code. The orchestrator takes the throne. Subagents are dispatched — each conjures a chamber on the dungeon map, complete with torchlight and purpose.
II
Explore
Watch agents inhabit their rooms in real time. Research agents gather scrolls in the library. Code agents brew in the laboratory. Reviewers inspect the armory.
III
Return
As agents complete their quests, chambers dim. Output flows back to the throne room through the corridors. The dungeon contracts. You witness the full lifecycle.
